game ready dragon rig


He is comprised of 32,256 triangles making up 24 objects controlled by 147 bones in a painstakingly full custom rig.


to get the surface details I wanted, I sculpted a very high poly version of his head, body and front legs in blender, and baked the normal information to the low poly mesh.


the final product is a hight fidelity low cost animatable character ready for use in almost every gameEngine.

MetaKnight Render



There is a story behind this one.
