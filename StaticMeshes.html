Night Shift

night shift was created for the 2024 brackeys GAMEJAM hosted on itch.io. I was the sole artist on the project, and everything was created from scratch in 1 week.

CREATURE : PROPS

Since all of these had to be made quickly, all texturing is done from either free internet images mapped via weird UVs, or modified and baked noise textures. the fireextinguisher is a prime example of where I grabbed a free image and wrapped it on the mesh, and the spikes albedo and roughness is a good example of where I used noise. In a 1 week project done is better than perfect. Everything ran smoothly and was finished quickly, though the quality is not quite what I wanted it to be.
